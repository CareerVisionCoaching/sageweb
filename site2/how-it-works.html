How Sage Works

Three simple phases guide your entire career journey: Plan, Execute, and Reflect.

Your Journey in Three Phases

Sage.coach streamlines your career progression with a proven methodology. Each phase builds on the previous one, creating momentum and clarity toward your goals.

1

Plan

Create Your Tailored Roadmap

Begin your journey by working with your coach to create a personalized roadmap. This isn't a one-size-fits-all template ‚Äî it's a custom plan designed specifically for your goals, experience level, and timeline.

What You'll Do:

  • Define clear, measurable career objectives
  • Set major milestones (e.g., Resume Update, Network Building, Interview Practice)
  • Break down milestones into actionable tasks
  • Establish realistic timelines and checkpoints

Why It Matters:

A structured roadmap eliminates ambiguity and provides both you and your coach with a shared vision. You'll always know what's next, why it matters, and how it contributes to your larger goal.

2

Execute

Work Your Plan in One Unified Workspace

With your roadmap in place, it's time to take action. Sage.coach provides a centralized workspace where you hold sessions, complete tasks, and collaborate with your coach ‚Äî all without switching between multiple tools.

What You'll Do:

  • Attend coaching sessions and log notes in real-time
  • Complete assigned tasks and track progress
  • Upload documents (resumes, cover letters, portfolios)
  • Communicate with your coach via integrated messaging
  • Update milestone status as you advance

Why It Matters:

Execution is where progress happens. By consolidating all your coaching activities in one place, you eliminate friction, stay organized, and maintain momentum even when life gets busy.

3

Reflect

Learn and Adapt with AI-Powered Insights

Progress isn't just about completing tasks ‚Äî it's about learning what works, what doesn't, and how to optimize your approach. Sage.coach uses AI to help you and your coach extract insights from your journey.

What You'll Do:

  • Review AI-generated session summaries
  • Analyze progress metrics and completion rates
  • Receive AI-suggested next steps based on your history
  • Identify patterns in successful vs. challenging tasks
  • Refine your roadmap based on learnings

Why It Matters:

Reflection turns experience into wisdom. By regularly reviewing your journey with AI-powered insights, you accelerate learning, avoid repeated mistakes, and continuously improve your strategy.

How It All Comes Together

These three phases create a continuous improvement cycle. You Plan your roadmap with clear milestones, Execute tasks in a unified workspace, and Reflect on your progress with AI-powered insights.

After each reflection phase, you refine your plan based on what you've learned. Milestones get updated, new tasks emerge, and your strategy adapts to changing circumstances.

The result? A dynamic, personalized journey that evolves with you ‚Äî not a rigid plan that becomes obsolete the moment reality intervenes.
