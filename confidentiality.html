Between Coach & Client Using Sage.coach

This Mutual Confidentiality Agreement ("Agreement") governs confidentiality between a Coach ("Coach") and a Client ("Client") using the Sage.coach platform ("Platform"). The purpose of this Agreement is to ensure that both parties maintain strict confidentiality regarding personal, professional, and proprietary information shared during the coaching relationship.

1. Definitions

"Confidential Information" means any non-public information shared by either party, including:

  • Personal details, employment history, job search status
  • Coaching notes, assessments, and feedback
  • Resumes, documents, assignments, and interview materials
  • Roadmaps, progress data, and analytics
  • Business methods, content, or proprietary frameworks
  • Communications (email, in-platform messages, session discussions)

Confidential Information may be shared verbally, digitally, or in written form.

2. Mutual Confidentiality Obligations

Both the Coach and Client agree to:

  • Keep all Confidential Information strictly private
  • Not share Confidential Information with third parties without written consent
  • Use Confidential Information solely for the purpose of coaching and professional development
  • Protect Confidential Information with reasonable security measures
  • Notify the other party if any unauthorized disclosure occurs

3. Exceptions

This Agreement does not apply to information that:

  • Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the receiving party
  • Was already lawfully known before disclosure
  • Is independently developed without reference to Confidential Information
  • Must be disclosed to comply with a legal, regulatory, or court order (the disclosing party will be notified where legally allowed)

4. Data Stored in Sage.coach

Both Coach and Client acknowledge:

  • Information stored in Sage.coach may be accessed by authorized users within the organization (Org Owners, Head Coaches, or Admins).
  • Sage.coach staff may access data only when necessary for support, maintenance, or legal compliance.
  • Sage.coach acts as a data processor and follows strict security, privacy, and access controls.
