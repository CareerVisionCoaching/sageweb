Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 17, 2025

Sage.coach ("Sage," "we," "our," or "us") is a software platform that helps coaching organizations manage clients, coaching sessions, job search activities, and related workflow tools. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and safeguard your information when you use sage.coach or any related services.

If you have any questions, contact us at: privacy@sage.coach

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in three ways:

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

  • Account information (name, email, password, role)
  • Organization details (org name, billing info, settings)
  • Client information added by coaches (name, email, resume files, notes, progress data)
  • Coaching session information (dates, notes, feedback)
  • Uploaded files (resumes, presentations, documents)
  • Payment and billing information
  • Communication messages (support tickets, feedback)
  • Integrations you authorize (e.g., calendar)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage logs (pages visited, actions performed)
  • Cookies and similar technologies
  • Timezone and location approximations

1.3 Information from Third-Party Integrations

If you connect Sage.coach to external services, we may receive:

  • Calendar events (name, date, participants)
  • File metadata from storage providers
  • Authentication tokens from identity providers

You control which integrations you connect and may revoke access at any time.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use data to:

  • Provide and maintain the Sage.coach platform
  • Personalize and optimize user experience
  • Track client progress and coaching workflows
  • Provide analytics to coaches and organizations
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Detect and prevent abuse, fraud, or security incidents
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve the platform and develop new features
  • Communicate product updates or relevant information (opt-out available)

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share information with:

3.1 Service Providers

Only as needed to operate Sage.coach:

  • Payment processors
  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Email and notification services
  • Analytics tools
  • Customer support tools
