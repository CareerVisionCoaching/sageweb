Why Structured Roadmaps Work (When They're Done Right)

The problem with most coaching "frameworks" is they're either too rigid (everyone gets the same thing) or too loose (you're reinventing everything). CoachOS roadmaps split the difference.

The Four Stages (Every Client, Every Time)

Structured enough to keep things on track. Flexible enough to adapt to each client's situation.

1

Stage 1: Discovery

Weeks 1-2

Core Milestones:

  • Values clarification - What actually matters to them
  • Skills inventory - What they're genuinely good at
  • Market positioning - Where their skills translate to opportunity
  • Career narrative - A coherent story they can tell
How CoachOS Makes This Work:
  • Flexible duration based on client readiness - system adapts timing
  • You customize which exercises fit their situation
  • System tracks completion and flags if they're stuck on a milestone
2

Stage 2: Strategy

Weeks 3-5

Core Milestones:

  • Target company research - Which organizations to pursue
  • Role alignment - Which positions match their profile
  • Application strategy - The plan for getting in the door
  • Networking plan - Who to connect with and how
How CoachOS Makes This Work:
  • Built-in decision frameworks you can deploy for company/role evaluation
  • Task dependencies keep work logically sequenced
  • AI suggests resources based on their specific targets
3

Stage 3: Execution

Weeks 6-11

Core Milestones:

  • Resume refinement - Materials that tell the right story
  • Application tracking - What's in progress, what needs follow-up
  • Interview prep - Practice, feedback, refinement
  • Negotiation prep - Ready before they get an offer
How CoachOS Makes This Work:
  • Real-time progress on each application - see what's moving, what's stalled
  • Interview frameworks organized by stage (phone screen, panel, final)
  • Follow-up reminders so nothing slips through the cracks
4

Stage 4: Close

Week 12+

Core Milestones:

  • Offer evaluation - Compare compensation, culture, growth
  • Negotiation execution - Get what they're worth
  • Transition planning - How to exit current role gracefully
  • Post-acceptance check-ins - Set up for success in new role
How CoachOS Makes This Work:
  • Decision matrices for evaluating multiple offers side-by-side
  • Negotiation scripts and frameworks ready to deploy
  • Transition preparation protocols for the first 30/60/90 days
