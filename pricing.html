Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to pay per coach? +

No. You can add unlimited coaches, assistants, or collaborators at no extra cost. Pricing is based entirely on the number of active clients.

What counts as an active client? +

Any client marked as Active, Interviewing, or In-Program. Archived or graduated clients do not count toward plan limits.

Can I upgrade or downgrade as I grow? +

Yes. Upgrades apply immediately; downgrades apply next billing cycle.

Will anything break if I exceed a plan limit? +

No. You simply won't be able to add new active clients until upgrading.

Is there a contract? +

No long-term commitments. Plans are monthly with an annual discount option.

Do you offer migration support? +

Yes. Growth and Impact plans include optional migration help from tools like Notion, Excel, or Airtable. Impact plans include full white-glove onboarding.

What happens after the trial ends? +

You'll choose a plan. Your data stays intact whether you subscribe or pause.

Are AI features optional? +

Yes. AI can be turned on/off per coach or per client.
