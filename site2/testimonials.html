Featured Success Story

Marcus Thompson

Senior Engineer ‚Üí Engineering Director

Timeline: 12 weeks | Salary Increase: 45%

The Challenge

After 8 years as a senior engineer, Marcus felt ready for leadership but didn't know how to position himself for director roles. His resume highlighted technical skills but lacked leadership narrative. He had applied to 15 positions with zero callbacks.

The Journey

Working with his Sage.coach mentor, Marcus followed a structured 5-milestone roadmap:

  • Foundation (Week 1-2): Reframed resume to highlight leadership impact, not just technical contributions
  • Network Building (Week 3-5): Conducted 12 informational interviews with current directors
  • Interview Prep (Week 6-8): Developed 20 leadership STAR stories using AI-assisted practice
  • Applications (Week 9-10): Targeted 8 director roles with customized materials
  • Negotiation (Week 11-12): Received 3 offers, negotiated final package

The Outcome

Marcus accepted a Director of Engineering role at a Series B startup. Key results:

  • 45% salary increase + equity package
  • Went from 0 callbacks to 3 offers in 12 weeks
  • Gained clarity on leadership positioning
  • Built a network of 20+ director-level contacts
