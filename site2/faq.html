General Questions

What is Sage.coach?
Sage.coach is an all-in-one platform for career coaching. It combines structured roadmaps, collaborative workspaces, and AI-powered tools to help mid-career tech professionals achieve their career goals faster and with greater clarity.
Who is Sage.coach for?
Sage.coach is designed for mid-career tech professionals seeking promotions, career transitions, or leadership development. It's also for career coaches who want to deliver better results with less administrative overhead.
Do I need to work with a coach to use Sage.coach?
While Sage.coach is most powerful when used with a coach, you can also use it independently to create your own roadmaps and track your progress. However, we recommend working with a certified coach for best results.
How is Sage.coach different from other career tools?
Unlike generic project management tools or scattered career resources, Sage.coach is purpose-built for career coaching. It integrates roadmaps, session logs, document management, AI assistance, and progress tracking in one platform ‚Äî designed specifically for the coach-client relationship.

Most clients see results within 3‚Äì6 months, but your coach will tailor the roadmap to your specific needs. Some focused goals (like interview prep) may take 4-8 weeks, while larger transitions (like career pivots) may take 6-12 months.

Platform & Features

What features does Sage.coach include?
Core features include:
  • Visual roadmaps with custom milestones and tasks
  • Session logging and note-taking
  • AI-generated session summaries and interview prep
  • Document storage and management
  • Progress tracking and analytics
  • Coach-client messaging and collaboration
  • Customizable templates for coaches
How does the AI assistance work?
Our AI features help in several ways: automatically generating session summaries, suggesting next steps based on your progress, helping prepare STAR stories for interviews, and providing personalized recommendations. The AI learns from your journey but never replaces the human coaching relationship.
Can I access Sage.coach on mobile devices?
Yes! Sage.coach is fully responsive and works on desktop, tablet, and mobile browsers. Native mobile apps for iOS and Android are currently in development and will be released in early 2025.
Does Sage.coach integrate with other tools?
Currently, we offer calendar integrations (Google Calendar, Outlook) for session scheduling. Team plan subscribers get API access for custom integrations. We're actively developing integrations with LinkedIn, Notion, and other popular productivity tools.
Can I customize the roadmap templates?
Yes! Coaches can create custom roadmap templates based on common use cases (promotion, career change, interview prep, etc.) and reuse them across clients. Each roadmap can then be further customized for individual needs.

Security & Privacy

Is my data secure?
Yes. Sage.coach is SOC 2 Type II certified and compliant with major data-protection standards including GDPR and CCPA. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We undergo regular third-party security audits.
Who can see my career data?
Only you and the coaches you've explicitly granted access to can see your data. We never sell or share your information with third parties. Even our support team requires your permission before accessing your account for troubleshooting.
Where is my data stored?
Data is stored on enterprise-grade servers in SOC 2-certified data centers with redundant backups. We use industry-leading cloud infrastructure providers with 99.99% uptime SLAs.
Can I delete my data?
Absolutely. You can delete individual items (notes, documents, tasks) at any time. If you close your account, all your data is permanently deleted within 30 days, in accordance with GDPR right-to-erasure requirements.
How does AI use my data?
AI features process your data to generate summaries and suggestions, but your data is never used to train AI models or shared with third parties. All AI processing happens in secure, isolated environments and adheres to strict privacy standards.

Coaching & Support

Does Sage.coach provide coaches, or do I bring my own?
Both options are available. You can work with your existing coach who uses Sage.coach, or we can connect you with certified coaches from our network. All network coaches are vetted professionals with proven track records.
Can I switch coaches?
Yes, you can change coaches at any time to find the best fit. Your roadmap and data remain with you, and you can grant access to a new coach whenever you're ready.
What kind of support does Sage.coach offer?
Support varies by plan:
  • Individual: Email support (24-48 hour response time)
  • Coach: Priority email and chat support (12-24 hour response time), plus onboarding and training
  • Team: Dedicated account manager, SLA-backed support (4-hour response time), custom training
Do you offer training for new users?
Yes! All Coach and Team plans include comprehensive onboarding and training. We offer live training sessions, video tutorials, and detailed documentation. Individual plan users have access to self-service resources and video guides.
What if I have a feature request?
We love hearing from our users! Submit feature requests through our feedback portal or directly to your account manager (Team plans). We review all requests and prioritize based on user impact and feasibility.

Billing & Accounts

How does billing work?
Billing is monthly or annual, depending on your plan choice. You'll be charged at the start of each billing cycle. Annual plans receive a 20% discount and are billed once per year.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel anytime. Your account will remain active until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees or penalties. You can export all your data before canceling.
What happens if my payment fails?
We'll notify you immediately if a payment fails and retry the charge after a few days. If payment continues to fail, your account will be paused (not deleted) after 14 days. You can reactivate by updating your payment method.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days, contact us for a full refund, no questions asked.
Can I get a discount for nonprofits or educational institutions?
Yes! We offer special pricing for registered nonprofits, educational institutions, and career services departments. Contact our sales team with your nonprofit or educational documentation for details.
