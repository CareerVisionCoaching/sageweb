Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after my 14-day trial? +

Your trial gives you full access to all features. No credit card required to start. At the end of 14 days, you can choose a plan that fits your needs. If you don't select a plan, your account will simply pause—no surprise charges.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period, and you can export all your data.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later? +

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate your billing automatically.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) through our secure payment processor, Stripe. Annual plans can also be paid via invoice for Enterprise customers.

Is my data secure? +

Yes. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (SSL/TLS). We're GDPR and CCPA compliant, and SOC 2 certification is in progress. You own your data and can export it anytime.

Do you offer refunds? +

Yes. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If Sage doesn't simplify your workflow or save you time within the first 30 days, we'll refund your payment—no questions asked.
