Our Story

The Problem

As an experienced career coach working with dozens of mid-career tech professionals, our founder noticed a recurring challenge: clients and coaches alike were drowning in scattered tools, disconnected conversations, and unclear progress metrics.

Email threads for session notes. Spreadsheets for task tracking. Separate documents for interview prep. Calendar apps for scheduling. The administrative overhead was stealing precious time from what really mattered ‚Äî the coaching itself.

The Insight

Every successful career transition followed a similar pattern: clear goal-setting, structured milestones, consistent accountability, and adaptive preparation. Yet there wasn't a single platform designed specifically to support this journey for both coaches and clients.

The insight was simple: what if we could combine the structure of project management, the collaboration of modern workspaces, and the intelligence of AI ‚Äî all tailored specifically for career coaching?

The Solution

Sage.coach was born from this insight. We built a platform that transforms career coaching from a collection of disparate activities into a unified, measurable journey.

Today, coaches use Sage to guide their clients through custom roadmaps with clear milestones. Clients see their progress in real-time, complete tasks in a centralized workspace, and leverage AI to prepare for interviews and next steps. Together, they achieve results faster and with greater clarity than ever before.

Our commitment remains the same: to continually improve the platform based on real feedback from coaches and clients, ensuring that Sage.coach evolves with the needs of the people it serves.
