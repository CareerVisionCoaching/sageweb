Questions Coaches Actually Ask

Can't find your answer? Email us or book a 15-minute walkthrough.

Is this for me or for my clients?
You. Sage is infrastructure for your coaching practice. Your clients see their roadmap and progress, but the platform is built to solve YOUR operational chaos - scattered notes, forgotten follow-ups, administrative overhead.
Will this make my coaching feel robotic or impersonal?
No. Sage makes your coaching more consistent and effective. The frameworks give you structure so you can focus on the human part. Think of it like a pilot's checklist - it doesn't make flying impersonal, it makes sure nothing critical gets missed.
What if my process is different from your roadmap stages?
The four Sage stages (Discovery, Strategy, Execution, Close) are high-level containers. You customize the milestones and tasks inside each stage to match your approach. The structure is there to keep you organized, not to dictate your methodology.
How much time does setup take?
First client roadmap takes maybe 30-45 minutes to customize from templates. After that, each new client is 10-15 minutes because you're reusing what works. Compare that to your current "start from scratch every time" approach.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
You export everything - all notes, roadmaps, client data. It's yours. We don't hold it hostage.
Is client data secure?
Yes. SOC 2 compliant, encrypted at rest and in transit, regular security audits. We take this seriously because your client relationships depend on it.
Can I try it with one client before committing?
That's what the 14-day trial is for. Bring in one or two clients, see if Sage makes your life easier. If it doesn't, you don't pay.
Do I need to change how I coach?
No. Sage adapts to your style, not the other way around. If you already use certain frameworks or exercises, drop them into Sage. If you don't, we have templates to start with.
What makes Sage different from just using Notion or Airtable?
Those are general-purpose tools. You're still building the infrastructure. Sage is purpose-built for career coaching workflow - client stages, milestone tracking, session prep, AI assistance. It's the difference between building your own house and moving into one designed for how you actually live.
Can I collaborate with other coaches?
Team plans include collaboration features - shared frameworks, cross-coach analytics, client handoffs. Useful if you run a practice or want to share approaches with peers.
